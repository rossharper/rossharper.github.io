Jim Marshall Tribute

Posted on Saturday, 7th April 2012

News broke that Jim Marshall, founder of Marshall Amplification, passed away this week. His iconic brand of guitar amplifiers have been used by many of the most famous guitarists around the world, and they have inspired many a teenager. I had half an hour to spare before heading to the pub on Thursday night, so I knocked together this small tribute.
